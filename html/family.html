All family members share equal part while shouldering responsibilities within the family. I am blessed to have them in my life. This is very much required in the society as because a good family makes a good society and a good society makes a good country.

A person will work happily, behave well with others, and completes all his/her responsibilities towards society and nation if they are backed up by their family. Behind every successfull being there is his/her family.
