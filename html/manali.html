It was an excellent experience when I with my friends visited MANALI for the first time. My wish was to watch the snow falling which I had never seen before any trip and destination. I had a booked trip through Go ibibo according to the weather forecast. We had explored the Solang valley on day two and enjoyed the live snow there. You can enjoy some snow activities at Solang valley like : Paragliding, Yark Ride, Snowboarding, Skiing.
