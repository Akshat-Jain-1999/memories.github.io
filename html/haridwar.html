As we grow old, childhood memories become the best part of our life. Undoubtedly, childhood can be termed as the heaven of a man’s life. Childhood is the time when we live like kings. We have no tensions; even if there are tensions, we don’t care for them. The prime motive of life during childhood is fun and joy. We run across fields, play continuously without caring for the time at all. As we grow, these memories about childhood, events and incidents shine like stars in the sky of our life. Hence everyone loves and cherishes childhood memories.
